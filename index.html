Spicy Braised Tofu

A quick, nutritious, and flavour-packed meal by Chef Lola's Kitchen

About This Recipe

This Spicy Braised Tofu recipe is big on taste. The beautiful blend of spices in the marinade perfectly complements the fried tofu and vegetables. It is a very fast, easy, and nutritious meal that can be served over rice, pasta, quinoa, or couscous — and it tastes even better the next day as the tofu absorbs all the flavour from the marinade.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4 people
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Cuisine: Asian-inspired

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm or firm tofu
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 stalks green onion, chopped
  • Half a cup of onion, diced
  • 4 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, for garnishing
  • A handful of snap peas

Marinade Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli flakes
  • Half to three-quarters of a cup of stock — chicken, beef, or vegetable all work
  • Salt to taste, optional depending on your stock
